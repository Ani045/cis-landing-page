FAQ's

  • How can I get a visa for medical treatment in India?

    Once you confirm your treatment with us, we will provide a visa requisition letter to facilitate your visa application. Please provide your details via email to start the process.

  • What languages do your interpreters speak?

    Our interpreters are fluent in multiple languages including Arabic, French, Russian, and more, to ensure effective communication throughout your stay.

  • Can Convicare help with accommodations for my family?

    Yes, we can arrange accommodations for your family or attendants according to your preferences, from budget options to luxury hotels.

  • What should I bring with me for my treatment at Convicare?

    Please bring all relevant medical documents, a valid passport, visa, travel insurance information, and personal items for your stay. Also, bring any medications you are currently taking.

  • How is the post-discharge process managed for international patients?

    Post-discharge, we assist with travel arrangements, provide tele-consultation services, and can organize a stay for recuperation in India, along with sightseeing if you are interested.

  • What payment methods does Convicare accept for international patients?

    ConviCare accepts various payment methods including wire transfers, major credit cards (VISA, MasterCard, AMEX), and direct payments at the hospital. We can also accommodate payments in multiple currencies.

  • Are there any cultural or religious accommodations available at Convicare?

    Yes, we respect the diverse backgrounds of our international patients. Convicare provides various cultural and religious accommodations, including halal meals, spaces for prayer, and language-appropriate religious materials.

  • Can Convicare arrange medical consultations or treatments that are not listed on the website?

    Absolutely. If you have specific medical needs, please discuss them with your international patient coordinator. We have a wide network of specialists across various disciplines and can arrange for specialized consultations and treatments as required.

  • How does Convicare ensure privacy and confidentiality for its international patients?

    We adhere strictly to patient confidentiality and privacy laws. All medical records, discussions, and treatments are handled with the utmost discretion and security.

  • What should I do if there is an emergency situation during my stay?

    ConviCare provides 24/7 emergency care. In case of an emergency, you can contact the hospital’s main line or speak directly to your international patient coordinator who will ensure rapid response and care.

  • How long do I need to stay in India after my treatment for follow-up care?

    The duration of stay post-treatment varies depending on the type of procedure and your recovery rate. Your doctor and international patient coordinator will provide a recommended timeline based on your specific treatment plan.

  • Can Convicare help with extending my medical visa if needed?

    Yes, our dedicated visa assistance team can help with the necessary documentation and procedures to apply for an extension of your medical visa if your treatment requires a longer stay.

  • Does Convicare provide support for rehabilitation services post-treatment?

    Yes, we offer comprehensive rehabilitation services including physical therapy, occupational therapy, and other specialized rehabilitation services tailored to your recovery needs.

  • Are there facilities for online consultations with doctors after I return to my country?

    ConviCare offers tele-consultation services enabling you to continue your follow-up appointments with your treating doctors from the comfort of your home country.

  • What amenities are available for attendants staying with the patient?

    Attendants can enjoy amenities such as comfortable lounge areas, cafeteria services, Wi-Fi, and accommodation assistance. We ensure that attendants are comfortable and well taken care of during their stay.
